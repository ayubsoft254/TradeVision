What is Two-Factor Authentication?

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring two different ways to prove your identity when logging in.

Benefits:

  • Protects against password theft
  • Reduces account takeover risk
  • Enhances trading security
  • Complies with security standards

How it works:

Enter your password as usual

2

Enter the 6-digit code from your phone

3

Access your secure account
